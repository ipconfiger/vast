Files modified/created:

- src/api/mod.rs — added channel_members module, nested routes under /channels
- src/main.rs — added `mod api;` and .merge(api::routes())

Endpoints registered (under /api):
  POST   /api/channels/{channel_id}/members        — add member (owner/admin)
  GET    /api/channels/{channel_id}/members         — list members (any member)
  DELETE /api/channels/{channel_id}/members/{user_id} — remove/leave (owner or self)
  PATCH  /api/channels/{channel_id}/members/{user_id}/role — update role (owner only)

Authorization logic:
  require_membership()        — checks channel_members table, 403 if not member
  require_role(allowed_roles) — checks role in channel_members, 403 if insufficient

Edge cases handled:
  - Owner cannot leave channel without transferring ownership first
  - Owner cannot remove another owner
  - Cannot change owner's role (except self-role-change by owner)
  - Only owners can grant owner/admin roles to new members
  - Role validation: must be one of owner/admin/member
  - 204 No Content on successful deletion
  - 403 Forbidden with descriptive messages for all permission failures

Verification: cargo test passes (only pre-existing errors in ws/mod.rs and missing channels.rs)
